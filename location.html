üí° Pro Tips for Finding Flying Locations

Use AirMap or B4UFLY: These apps show real-time airspace information and help you find legal flying spots near you.

Join local Facebook groups: Connect with other Bay Area and Central Valley drone pilots who share their favorite locations.

Scout locations ahead of time: Visit potential flying spots without your drone first to assess safety, crowds, and conditions.

Respect "No Drone" signs: If a location explicitly prohibits drones, honor those rules even if airspace is technically clear.
